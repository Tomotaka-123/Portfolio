Course Title

Class: Ethical Hacking

Semester: Spring 2023

Description:This course is designed to provide an introduction to the field of ethical hacking. Students will learn the fundamentals of network security and explore various tools and techniques used by hackers to penetrate networks. The course will cover a range of topics including server review, hacking video courses, pen testing, and ethics in hacking. Throughout the semester, students will engage in Try Hack Me Labs to apply their knowledge and gain hands-on experience. In addition, students will watch and analyze pre-recorded video courses by Network Chuck and Oâ€™reilly to learn about various hacking techniques and tools. The course will culminate with a Demonstrations Exam that will test the student's understanding of the material covered in the video courses and Try Hack Me Labs. Additionally, students will be required to submit an Ethics paper on hacking and its impact on society. By the end of the course, students will have a foundational understanding of ethical hacking and be equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to perform basic pen testing.
